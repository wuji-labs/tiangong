**Your AI designs with half a palette. Give it the other half.**

Most AI design training is dominated by Western frameworks — Material Design, Bauhaus, Swiss Style. Clean, minimal, modern... but often soulless.

**TianGong** (天工) infuses 5000 years of Chinese aesthetic wisdom into AI design. Not as decoration, but as a complete philosophical system for understanding beauty, space, rhythm, and meaning.

## The Problem

```
You: "Design a landing page"
AI without TianGong: Perfect grid. Perfect spacing. Perfect... nothing.
AI with TianGong: A page that breathes. That flows like water.
                  Where empty space speaks louder than content.
                  Where every color carries meaning.
```

## What It Teaches AI

### 🎨 The Six Principles (谢赫六法, 5th century)
The world's oldest surviving design framework:

| Principle | Chinese | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Spirit Resonance | 气韵生动 | Design must feel **alive** |
| Structural Brushwork | 骨法用笔 | Beauty needs **bones** |
| Faithful to Nature | 应物象形 | Understand before designing |
| Meaningful Color | 随类赋彩 | Color = meaning, not decoration |
| Governing Space | 经营位置 | Empty space IS the design |
| Learning Masters | 传移模写 | Study tradition, then transcend |

### 🧠 Five Wisdoms (五智) — Decision Framework

| Wisdom | Source | Ask Yourself |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| 道法自然 | Dao De Jing Ch.25 | Does it feel natural? |
| 大巧若拙 | Dao De Jing Ch.45 | Am I showing off or serving? |
| 器以载道 | Yi Jing | What meaning does it carry? |
| 天人合一 | Yi Jing | Does it harmonize with context? |
| 中庸之道 | Zhongyong | Is it balanced? |

### 🏺 Material Wisdom — From 4 Traditions

| Tradition | Teaches | Design Application |
|-----------|---------|-------------------|
| 书法 Calligraphy | Rhythm, tension, flow | Typography, visual rhythm |
| 水墨 Ink Painting | Layers, emptiness, life | Visual hierarchy, whitespace |
| 园林 Gardens | Discovery, framing, sequence | Navigation, progressive disclosure |
| 瓷器 Porcelain | Restraint, hidden beauty, form | Color palettes, surface design |

### 🏛️ Song Dynasty Standard (宋代美学, 960-1279)

The peak of Chinese refined taste — 800 years before Dieter Rams:

```
极简 Radical simplicity
素雅 Elegant plainness
格物 Investigate before creating
理趣 Delight in principle
天然 Naturalness above artifice
```
